Welcome to Coffee Central

In 2023 four coffee loving students decided to create there very own coffee company called Coffee central.
They decides to create this company becausee they were fed up with the prices and quality of the coffee around there campus.

Our coffee beans are souced only from the continent of africa directly from the farms in Ethopia, Tanzania and the DRC.
Our beans are roasted and smoked with rooikrans wood before it is ground up packaged.
These ground up beans create a deep rich and flavorful cup of coffee to extinguish that crave you have been feeling.
